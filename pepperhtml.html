Comparing Hot Peppers around the World

There are over 400 peppers in the world of which very few are known. With this visualization we want to compare peppers from around the world, to show the relationship between the spiciness of the peppers and their color, origin, or species. Our goal is to introduce you to a wide selection of peppers that you can incorporate to your diet.
