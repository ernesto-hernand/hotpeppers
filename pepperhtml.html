Comparing Hot Peppers around the World

There are over 400 peppers in the world of which very few are known. Most of the hottest peppers tend to be orange to red in color. Regions with most of the hottest peppers are Asia and the Americas, such as India and the US. The pepper species Chinense dominates the rest with a majority of the hottest peppers. It's incredible how spicy, numerically, peppers can get!
