Comparing Hot Peppers around the World

With this visualization, we want to compare a few dozen of the hottet peppers in the world. We also want to show which continents/countries cultivate the most and hottest peppers. In addition, we want the visualization to provide insight on the many variations of peppers that humans made and consumed
